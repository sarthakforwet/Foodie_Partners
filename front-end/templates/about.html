THE ANNOTATORS

Coming from six different states of our country, India and united by Mody University of Science and Technology, Rajasthan, The Annotators comprises of 6 young women who believe in the power of hardwork, diligence and hope to use their knowledge in making the world a better and easier place to live in. Having faced with some of the most common and inevitable issues during our own Engineering career, we set on a journey with a positive attitude and a technologically driven idea to provide a solution to those who have come across similar dilemma during their careers in life.

Although all 6 of us are pursuing Bachelors in Technology, CSE, from our institution, we specialize in our own fields and always had 100s of crazy ideas for the same problem. We would often give up on the problem but nevertheless, start all over again challenging our own selves each time.

It surely wasn't a smooth ferry ride but a roller coaster that went higher up in the sky each time after it came crashing down.

ABOUT OUR INNOVATION

With the advancements and upgradations in technology every 2 minutes, it has become a little difficult and a tedious task to cope up with the new market trends. We often upgrade ourselves with the trends but mostly, we find ourselves choosing between the career options we can go for based on our diverse skill set.

This is an initiative where the young minds struggling to decide amongst the best career options can get a platform to do so. They can find upto 5 different categories or job roles along with their respective probabilities that they can choose from based on the skills that they possess and their experience and expertise in the given field.

Our user friendly front-end let's you enter your skills and experiences and then it takes the help of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning models to predict the best and top 5 categories for you to choose from.

Apart from this, you will also get a detailed report which gives you an analysis of your skills and what else is needed in order to be better in those top 5 of your predictions. There is also a dedicated analysis of the current market trends of different job roles based on factors like Location, Salary and Skills required.

We hope that this initiative will help you in your struggle to find the best career option and hone your skills along the way.
